HOURGLASS BODY SHAPE

Ideal and famous for Hollywood bombshells since the 1950s, the "Hourglass Body Shape" is the single most iconic female body shape.

Hourglass figures can be any size, including a plus-size Hourglass Body Shape, but curves define the figure. Your shoulders and chest are full, your waist is slim, and your waist is round.

Also, have a waist circumference that is at least 25% smaller than your shoulder or bust measurements with an Hourglass Body Shape. Additionally, your waist is at least 25% smaller than your hips and your shoulder and hip measurements are within 5% of each other.

Your body is shaped like an hourglass. Unlike the Apple or Pear Body Shapes, the weight is not concentrated in one area of the body. Many young women have an Hourglass Body Shape.

Because of your low belly fat accumulation, you are much less likely to suffer from diabetes, heart disease, and cancer than women with an "Apple Body Shape" type.
