Exercieses for Inverted Triangle Body Shape










Workout guide for Inverted Triangle body shape:

  • Lower body workouts, including Squatting, lunging, and deadlifts will be beneficial for your body type and are great for achieving increased muscle mass and tone in the legs and glutes.

  • Indulge more in twisting type exercises that will improve shape and tone around the midsection and also helps sculpt & shape oblique stomach muscles.

  • Workouts should also include low weights and high repetition to ensure nice tone and shape without increasing the size of the muscles.

    		•


    Diet guide for Inverted Triangle body shape:

  • High-end protein diets: Fish, white meat, beans, legumes are all protein rich and a perfect solution to keep your body in shape. Have foods that low in fats and oils, rich in proteins and low in carbohydrates or starch.

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables: Whether you need a quick breakfast or need to satisfy your snack urges- go for fresh stuff. Diced apples, macadamia nuts, cucumbers, avocados, lettuce, peppers, celery sticks, and homemade energy bars are great for you.

  • Dairy Products: Have low-fat cheese, curd, butter, and milk. Just do not add many sweeteners except for brown sugar or honey and enjoy dairy. If you are allergic to dairy, go for almond milk or coconut milk or soya milk if you like the taste.
