RECTANGLE BODY SHAPE

Exercieses for Rectangle Body Shape

If your body isn't on the curvy side and your shoulders and hips are approximately the same measurements, you're likely a "Rectangle Body Shape".

A rectangle is sometimes called a "Banana Body Shape" or a straight body shape. You may have an athletic build, or you may be a rectangular body shape in size, but the common characteristic of a rectangle is that the shoulders and hips are about the same width apart.

The Rectangular Body Shape is sometimes called the ruler or straight body shape. With a Rectangular Body Shape type, your waist circumference is more than 25% smaller than your shoulders or bust.

You have a narrow frame and may be flat in the chest and chest. With a Rectangular Body Shape, you carry weight proportionally but can carry weight around your abdomen.

Many long-distance runners and dancers have this shape. Although you generally look lean, you may struggle to build lean muscle. This can sometimes slow down your metabolism.
